DMARC rollout to reduce spoofing risk

High-level summary (sanitised). No sensitive DNS records or internal domain details are included.

Problem

Email spoofing risk and inconsistent alignment across mail sources.

Approach

  • Inventory mail-sending sources and validate SPF/DKIM alignment.
  • Implement DMARC policy in phases (monitor → enforce) with reporting review.
  • Coordinate with cloud/mail stakeholders and document changes.

Outcome

  • Improved email authentication posture.
  • Reduced spoofing exposure and clearer governance over mail sources.

What I’d do next

  • Automate reporting review and alert on alignment regressions.
  • Periodic vendor/source re-validation.
