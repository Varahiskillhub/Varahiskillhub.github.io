Vulnerability management: coverage + SLA reporting

A repeatable approach to coverage, operations, and reporting that meets audit/SLA expectations.

Problem

Keeping scan coverage reliable, findings actionable, and SLA reporting accurate (Cyber Essentials 14-day, PCI ASV).

Approach

  • Asset tagging/taxonomy for ownership and reporting.
  • Coverage checks + scan scheduling operations.
  • Exception workflow (recast / accepted) with audit trail.
  • Exec reporting: ageing, SLA status, top recurring issues.

Outcome

  • Improved visibility and predictable remediation cadence.
  • Cleaner reporting for governance and compliance stakeholders.

Notes

  • Where possible, I prioritise exploitability + business impact, not raw counts.
  • Fix verification is part of the workflow (not optional).
